Dear friends,

The IZOLYATSIA team wishes you peaceful holidays, a Happy New Year and a Merry Christmas to those who celebrate it!

This year, we conducted cultural projects and programs to support Ukrainian local communities all around Ukraine and abroad. Through our programs, we have witnessed beautiful cultural and social initiatives, strength of empathy, mutual help and support, and a strong desire to help everyone suffering from the Russian invasion of Ukraine. These days, we celebrate the resilience of the Ukrainian people and believe that victory, rebuilding and homecoming will arrive soon.

We are grateful to everyone who defends Ukraine and helps the military and civilians. We also thank all our partners and friends for their support and cooperation.

See you in 2024!
